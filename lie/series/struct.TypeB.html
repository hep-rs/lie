The \(B_{n}\) infinite series of Lie groups.

The \(B_{n}\) (\(n \geq 2\)) series is also denoted as \(\mathrm{SO}(2n+1)\) and corresponds to the special orthogonal group of odd dimension which is the group of \((2n + 1) \times (2n + 1)\) orthogonal matrices with determinant 1.

The Cartan matrix for \(B_{n}\) is of the form

\begin{equation} \begin{pmatrix} 2 & -1 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ -1 & 2 & -1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 & 2 & -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 & 2 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & -1 & 2 \end{pmatrix} \end{equation}

Exceptional Isomorphisms

  • \(B_{1} \cong A_{1}\).
